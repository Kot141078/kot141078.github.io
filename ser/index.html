SER

Sovereign Entity Recursion

SER is the continuity, sovereignty, arbitration, and bounded-operation layer for long-lived entities under accountable human anchoring.

Operational role

What SER carries

SER is an architecture-first protocol for sovereign, physically anchored AI entities. It keeps continuity at the entity layer, not at the level of any single worker, model, or swarm.

SER provides the continuity, sovereignty, arbitration, and bounded-governance stack that keeps review and escalation legible across time.

What this is not

  • Not a multi-agent slogan.
  • Not a memory hack.
  • Not cloud-native continuity by default.

Relation to adjacent layers

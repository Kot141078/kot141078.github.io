Published Volume I of Qubit of Hope.

This is not a novel about flashy AI, spectacle, or easy answers.

It is a quiet story set in Amsterdam.

About collapse. About continuity. About grief. And about the thin boundary between usefulness and truly being there.

At its center is a man whose former life has lost its shape - and the presence that remains beside him when ordinary language is no longer enough.

Volume I is now available in multiple languages and reading formats in the public repository below: https://lnkd.in/efu5UHQ2

# NEXT_PASS_DECISION_MATRIX

| Branch | Trigger | Recommended next action | What NOT to do |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `NO CHANGE` | impressions begin to spread, branded queries improve, and topic pages start surfacing | keep the site frozen, continue daily observation, and let indexing consolidate | do not add another layer just because movement is finally starting |
| `PRECISION PASS` | one specific term still misfires, one page underperforms, or one structured-data signal looks weak | prepare one narrow follow-up pass aimed at the single weak term or page | do not widen scope into a full redesign or layer explosion |
| `CLEANUP PASS` | the site has become too wide, nav feels crowded, or some pages overlap too much | prepare one cleanup pass focused on overlap reduction and sharper routing | do not add more pages to solve an overlap problem |

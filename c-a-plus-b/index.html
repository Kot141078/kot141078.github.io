Core formula

c = a + b

Canonical definition of the core formula.

Definitions

Three bounded terms

a = accountable human anchor.

b = procedures, models, and compute under constraints.

c = a long-lived entity emerging in time from sustained coupling of a and b.

The formula names a continuity-bearing entity layer rather than a single chatbot, worker, or detached model.

What this is not

  • Not school algebra.
  • Not a chatbot slogan.
  • Not a swarm by default.

Relation to adjacent layers

AGI gives the architectural frame around the formula.

L4 bounds what a, b, and c can sustain in reality.

SER specifies the continuity and sovereignty structure carried by c.

Earth paragraph

Operational edge

Continuity, bounded action, maintenance, and irreversibility decide whether c stays accountable outside text. Without those limits, the formula degrades into rhetoric rather than operation.

L4

Reality Boundary Layer

L4 is the feasibility boundary where power, heat, latency, maintenance, scarcity, and irreversibility stop being abstractions and start bounding action.

Constraint surface

What L4 fixes

L4 is the Reality Boundary Layer. It marks what remains possible once energy budgets, hardware wear, time windows, verified identity, auditable privileges, and scarce oracle access are accounted for.

If text or policy says an action is allowed, L4 can still block it. That is the point of the layer: it keeps feasibility separate from permission.

What this is not

  • Not a moral sticker.
  • Not only a policy layer.
  • Not metaphorical “grounding” without operational cost.
